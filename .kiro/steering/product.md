# Product Overview

PREET_ENGLISH is a next-generation AI-powered English learning platform specifically designed for Hindi speakers. The platform combines modern pedagogy with cutting-edge AI to transform passive learning into active speaking proficiency.

## Core Mission
Empowering Hindi speakers to speak English with confidence and cultural nuance through immersive, bilingual learning experiences.

## Key Features
- **Triple-Deep AI Integration**: Native speaking practice with real-time feedback, immersive video calls with Saraswati AI tutor, and real-time story generation
- **Cultural Relevance**: Saraswati mascot, Hindi-first approach, and cultural context integration
- **Comprehensive Content**: 1625+ interactive lessons across 17 categories, 88 vocabulary words with Hindi translations, 25 speaking topics
- **Gamification**: XP system, levels, streaks, leaderboards, achievements, and daily goals
- **Bilingual Support**: Full Hindi readability with translations for all content and instructions

## Target Audience
Hindi speakers seeking to improve their English proficiency, from beginners to advanced learners, with emphasis on practical conversation skills and cultural adaptation.

## Quality Standard
Grade 9+ technical standard with premium "Hulu-Green" UI design, ensuring professional-grade learning experiences comparable to leading language learning platforms.
