## Implemented Lessons (Weeks 1-4)

### Week 1: Foundation Assessment
- **Lesson Title:** Self-Assessment and Goal Setting
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Vocabulary Items:** 15 (with Hindi translations)
- **Learning Objectives:** Help students assess their current English level and set achievable goals
- **Key Vocabulary:** Assessment, Proficiency, Fluency, Confidence, Goal, Milestone, Improvement, Challenge, Strength, Weakness, Progress

### Week 2: Basic Conversational Skills
- **Lesson Title:** Everyday Greetings and Introductions
- **Difficulty:** Beginner
- **Vocabulary Items:** 15 (with Hindi translations)
- **Learning Objectives:** Master common greetings and introduction phrases used in daily interactions
- **Key Vocabulary:** Greeting, Introduce, Name, Pleasure, Colleague, Friend, Formal, Informal, Address, Title

### Week 3: Intermediate Conversational Skills
- **Lesson Title:** Asking Questions and Showing Interest
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Vocabulary Items:** 15 (with Hindi translations)
- **Learning Objectives:** Learn to ask appropriate questions and show genuine interest in conversations
- **Key Vocabulary:** Question, Curious, Interested, Wonder, Experience, Opinion, Suggestion, Reason, Purpose

### Week 4: Expressing Opinions
- **Lesson Title:** Expressing Opinions and Agreement
- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Vocabulary Items:** 15 (with Hindi translations)
- **Learning Objectives:** Learn to express opinions respectfully and show agreement or disagreement appropriately
- **Key Vocabulary:** Opinion, Agree, Disagree, Think, Believe, Feel, Perspective, Viewpoint, Stance, Support
