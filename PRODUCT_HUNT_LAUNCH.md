## 💬 RESPONSES TO COMMON QUESTIONS

### "How is this different from Duolingo?"
```
Great question! While Duolingo is excellent, Preet English is specifically designed for Hindi speakers:

1. Hindi-first design with cultural context
2. 1625+ lessons (more comprehensive)
3. Audio on EVERY piece of content
4. No ads, no paywalls, no premium tiers
5. Works completely offline
6. Open source - community can contribute
7. Focused solely on English for Hindi speakers

Think of it as Duolingo + Anki + Memrise, but free, open source, and culturally relevant!
```

### "Is this really free?"
```
Yes! 100% free forever. No ads, no premium tiers, no hidden costs.

It's open source (MIT license) and built as a public good. The goal is to make English accessible to every Hindi speaker in India.

You can even self-host it if you want!
```

### "Does it work offline?"
```
Absolutely! It's a Progressive Web App (PWA), so:
- Install it like a native app
- Works completely offline
- Syncs when you're back online
- No app store needed

Perfect for areas with limited internet connectivity!
```

### "Can I contribute?"
```
Yes! We'd love your contributions:
```
