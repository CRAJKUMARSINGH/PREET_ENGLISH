## Beginner Level (230 Lessons)

### Core Topics & Cultural Context
1. **Greetings & Introduction**
   - Basic greetings across cultures
   - Cultural norms in introductions
   - Formality levels in different English-speaking countries

2. **Daily Activities**
   - Daily routines worldwide
   - Time concepts across cultures
   - Common gestures and their meanings

3. **Numbers & Basic Counting**
   - Number systems and formats
   - Currency and measurements
   - Phone numbers and addresses globally

### Assessment Checkpoints
- **After Each Module**: 5-10 question quiz
- **Mid-Level Project**: Create a personal introduction video
- **Final Assessment**: Simulated real-life scenarios

## Assessment Framework

### Progress Tracking
- Weekly quizzes (10%)
- Monthly projects (30%)
- Speaking assessments (20%)
- Final comprehensive exam (40%)

### Certification Levels
- Basic Proficiency (Beginner)
- Independent User (Intermediate)
- Proficient User (Advanced)
