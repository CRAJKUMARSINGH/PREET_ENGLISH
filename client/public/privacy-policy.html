Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Preet English is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Learning Progress: Stored locally in your browser
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics via Vercel
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, or personal information

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the learning experience
  • To track anonymous usage patterns for app improvement
  • To ensure app security and performance

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your progress is stored in your browser
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't store your personal data on our servers
  • Cookies: We use minimal cookies for app functionality

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts: For typography (privacy-focused loading)
  • Vercel: For hosting and serverless functions

5. Your Rights

  • Clear your browser data to remove all stored information
  • Use the app without creating accounts
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through our GitHub repository.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Check this page for the latest version.
